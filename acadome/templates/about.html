About AcaDome

We are a digital publisher of open access research articles.

Our principles

  • Maintain absolute open access.
    • Neither paywalls nor registration.
  • Centralise the fields of research in a database without journals.
    • In light of the irrelevance of multiple journals for a single subfield.
  • Streamline the editorial workflow.
    • From drafted manuscript to published article in 20 days.
  • Maintain a transparent financial model.
    • The article publishing fee is € 100.
  • Give back to the researchers.
    • Peer reviewers are paid € 10 per article.
  • Promote collaboration with academia (ARC).
    • Partner with institutes, universities, and consortiums.

privacy policy

For researchers

  • Affiliation with an institute is not necessary.
  • You will retain copyright of/ all rights to your article.
  • Your article will be published under the CC BY license.
  • Retraction of your article is subject to the editor's discretion.

When you send us your manuscript, you accept our publishing agreement.
If you have any queries, please contact us.

AcaDome Research Council (ARC)

ARC establishes a bridge between the academic and publishing industries. Each partner institute will elect a researcher as their representative in the council, alongside our founding directors. This provides a direct correspondence between researchers and the management of AcaDome.
